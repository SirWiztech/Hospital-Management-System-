Capabilities

Everything your facility needs

From admission to discharge and beyond, Vitalis covers every touchpoint in modern patient care delivery — compliant with TEFCA and FHIR R4 standards.

Patient Records

FHIR R4-compliant digital profiles with medical history, allergies, prescriptions, and lab results — fully searchable with AI-assisted summarization in seconds.

Smart Scheduling

ML-driven booking that reduces no-shows by up to 40%, integrates telehealth slots automatically, and optimizes provider availability in real time.

Billing & Invoicing

Automated medical coding, insurance claim submission with real-time eligibility checks, payment tracking, and value-based care reporting — zero paperwork.

Secure Data

AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access control, comprehensive audit logs, and HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time visualizations for bed occupancy, revenue cycle metrics, patient flow patterns, DRG analysis, and department-level KPIs — all customizable.

Care Coordination

HIPAA-compliant internal messaging, department channels, critical alert escalation chains, and care team assignment — ensuring the right person gets the right info.

Report Generator

One-click discharge summaries, CDA-formatted lab reports, referral letters, and continuity-of-care documents — branded, signed, and ready to transmit.

Smart Notifications

Multi-channel alerts via SMS, push, and email for appointment reminders, critical lab values, medication updates, and follow-up schedules — with priority escalation.
